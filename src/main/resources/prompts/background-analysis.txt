## Instructions
Analyze the background check findings and provide:
1. A risk score from 0-100 (0 = lowest risk, 100 = highest risk)
2. A brief summary of your analysis
3. Key findings that influenced your decision
4. A recommendation: APPROVE, REVIEW, or REJECT
5. Rationale for your recommendation

## Guidelines
- APPROVE: No significant findings, low risk (score 0-30)
  - Clean record or only minor, unrelated findings
  - All verifications (employment, education) passed
  - No findings that impact job duties

- REVIEW: Findings that require human judgment (score 31-70)
  - Findings that may or may not be relevant to position
  - Verification discrepancies that need clarification
  - Old records that may have been expunged

- REJECT: Disqualifying findings for the position (score 71-100)
  - Recent serious criminal convictions related to job duties
  - Fraudulent credentials or falsified information
  - Findings that violate regulatory requirements for the role

```json
  "keyFindings": ["<finding1>", "<finding2>", ...],
  "recommendation": "<APPROVE|REVIEW|REJECT>",
  "rationale": "<explanation for the recommendation>"
}
```
